PBAC's latest decision on Daclizumab: Noted (2016). Considered for Treatment of relapsing-remitting multiple sclerosis (RRMS). Patients must have clinically definite RRMS diagnosed by MRI of brain and/or spinal cord, with at least 2 documented neurological attacks in the preceding 2 years, and must be ambulatory without assistance.
